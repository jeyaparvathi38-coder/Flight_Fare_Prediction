AI Generated Insights

Our advanced analytics engine has processed the historical flight dataset to extract the following actionable insights to help you book smarter.

Best Booking Period

Booking flights 1-2 months in advance generally yields the lowest prices. Last-minute bookings (within 7 days) show an average price surge of 40%.

Cheapest Airlines

IndiGo and SpiceJet consistently offer the most competitive fares for domestic routes, especially for non-stop flights between major metro cities.

Expensive Routes

The Bangalore (BLR) to New Delhi (DEL) route exhibits the highest price volatility. Business class fares on Jet Airways for this route are significantly higher than the median.

Peak Travel Times

Flights departing between 6:00 AM and 9:00 AM, and 5:00 PM and 8:00 PM tend to have a 15% premium due to high business traveler demand.

Stops vs. Price

Interestingly, 1-stop flights are sometimes cheaper than non-stop flights for long distances, but 2+ stops generally increase both duration and cost.

Seasonal Trends

March and May witness the highest average fares due to holiday seasons and festival rush. September is generally the cheapest month to travel.
